Describing Relationships

Content for Tuesday, February 28, 2023

Required readings

  • Chapter 3 in Thinking Clearly with Data (Huntington-Klein 2021)

  • Chapter 4 in The Effect (Huntington-Klein 2021)

Guiding questions

  • What does it mean that there is a relationship, or correlation, between two variables?
  • What is a conditional distribution?
  • How can we quantify the correlation? How do we interpret these measures?
  • What are the limitations of describing a relationship with a correlation?

Coding

As for coding, this week we will carry on with plotting and do some data wrangling and reshaping as well. If not before the class, you can have a look at this chapter while working on the homework assignment.
