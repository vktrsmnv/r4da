Describing Variables

Content for Tuesday, February 21, 2023

Required readings

  • Chapter 3 in The Effect (Huntington-Klein 2021)

Guiding questions

  • What is a variable? What are the types of variables do we usually work with in social sciences?
  • What is a distribution of a variable? What is the difference between the distributions of continuous and discrete variables?
  • Why may we want to summarize a distribution? How can we do it?
  • What is variation of a variable? How can we measure it?
  • What is the difference between an empirical and a theoretical distribution? How do they both relate to the data generation process?
  • What are the characteristics of a normal distribution? What kind of processes can generate it?
